Ripple

Social Media of Music

Ripple is a new form of social media that takes music interactivity to the next level by connecting people all around the world through shared musical interests and experiences. This application represents a new way to share, listen to, and experience the music that you love and are passionate about.

User Research (Interview)

I interviewed a friend and avid music lover, Tylynn Lawson, to get ideas for this project:

What do you like about your music streaming services and what do you not like?

Tylynn Lawson: I like that I can have a playlist and then I pretty much look for a song and then I have it. And that I don’t have to pay for individual songs. I don’t like that some of the artists that you like or less popular songs aren't on there and you have to turn to something else to find it.

Why is it annoying that everything’s not in the same place?

T: Because when you make a playlist for something you can't always get to what you want, and then when you drive you would have to switch apps to find whatever song that you like and that can be dangerous.

Why would it be dangerous to switch apps when driving?

T: Because you're not focusing on driving and if you are using GPS, you can’t stream and use GPS at the same time so it makes things difficult.

What do you think would help streaming and driving?

T: Listening to music is fine, but if they had everything in one place then it'd be much better.

What if there was a streaming service that combined the libraries of all the services you were subscribed in one application?

T: That would be lit.

In conclusion I felt an app with YouTube music streaming capabilities and voice command would be very helpful as a music streaming service.
